What is Dution Pure

Dution Pure is the combination of Oil Trap System and WTP. It is used to recycle the kitchen water for other waste usages. Your used kitchen water turns clean, reusable water. kitchen water flows through the sink contains lot of unwanted solids like fat, oil, grease and suspended solids. After all these processes, we get we get theclear water for reuse.
