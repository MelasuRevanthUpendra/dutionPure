History of Senthil Aandavar

Kitchen water flows through the sink contains lot of unwanted solids like fat, oil, grease and suspended solids.

All these flow through the pipe and settles in the storage tank. Water and all the remaining particles starts filling in the tank. Due to high density of oil, the oil risen up, and the remaining particles settled down.

After that with the help of pump, water moves into conical tank. In this conical tank using diffusion process for 2 hours, the sludge gets into the bottom and the remaining water moves into another small tank.

Then the water enters into sand filter and ultra filter for filtration. After all these processes, we get the clear water for reuse.

How we invented Dution Pure?

The presence of Fat, Oil and Grease pollutes the water bodies and environment. To prevent these problems, Oil trap system is essential.
